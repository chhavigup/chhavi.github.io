About Me

I discovered my passion for software engineering early on, witnessing its transformative impact on our daily lives. This, combined with being a first-generation college student, inspired me to pursue a computer science degree. After graduating, I worked for three years as a software engineer, gaining valuable experience in creating enterprise software solutions.

My eagerness to learn and embrace new technologies prompted me to pursue a master's in computer science. I decided to move to the USA, where companies are building future of technology, revolutionizing how we operate.

Computer Science has progressed at a staggering speed over the past two decades, but for me it evolved from a fascination to a powerful tool that I use to challenge my creativity, analytical skills, and logical thinking as I solve real-world problems.When I'm not coding, I express my creativity through painting and dance. From childhood curiosity to a creative problem solver, I'm on a mission to push the boundaries of technology.
